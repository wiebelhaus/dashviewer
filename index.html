DashViewer

An Android companion app for VIOFO dashcams — view, download, and manage your footage over WiFi.

About

DashViewer connects directly to your VIOFO dashcam over its local WiFi network, letting you browse footage, watch a live camera feed, download clips, and manage camera settings — all from your phone, without removing the SD card.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

DashViewer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its developer or to any third party. All communication happens directly between your phone and your dashcam over your local WiFi connection — nothing is sent over the internet or to any server the developer operates.

Permissions

  • Location (precise): Required by Android in order to scan for and identify nearby WiFi network names (SSIDs), so the app can find and connect to your dashcam. DashViewer does not access, store, or share your device's actual GPS location.
  • WiFi / network state: Used to detect, connect to, and communicate with your dashcam's WiFi access point.
  • Internet: Used only to communicate with your dashcam over your local network. DashViewer does not make any requests to the public internet.

GPS data embedded in video files

Some dashcam recordings contain GPS coordinates recorded by the camera itself while filming. If you use DashViewer's map feature, this data is read directly from the video file on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Data storage

Footage you choose to download is saved to your device's local storage. You control deletion of this data through the app or your device's file manager.

Third parties

DashViewer does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking services.
